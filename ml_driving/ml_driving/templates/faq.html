FAQs

When can I start driving lessons?
You can legally start driving on the roads with an instructor, or legally eligible adult, once you have reached the age of 17 and possess a UK Provisional Driving License.
How many lessons will I need?
Learning to drive can be a variable procces. Everyone is different, therefore there is no specific answer. The recommended average is between 35 and 40 hours of professionally tutored lessons, however, everyone learns at their own pace and in their own way. After a few lessons, we can begin to make an informed prediction based on your individual performance and capacity to learn.
How much will it cost?
This will vary by individual. The overall cost will depend on the number of hours of lessons you take, and the effort you put into them, as well as how many times you may need to take the Theory and Practical tests. We endeavour to make the process as economical as possible by making the most out of the lessons you recieve by tailoring them to your individual strengths & weaknesses, and building on your skills as you progress. For details of our current prices, please see the section on pricing. Details of the current fees for tests and a provisional licence can be found in the section labelled, "Your Journey."
How long will lessons take?
Each lesson will be a minimum of one hour, however, it will be possible to book lessons that last a longer period of time.
Should I learn manual or automatic?
This is entirely up to you, however we would encourage you to learn to drive a manual vehicle. You can transfer these skills to driving an automatic very easily as the main difference is that the vehicle will change gears for you. The rest of the skills needed are essentially the same.

If you learn only to drive an automatic, you will not legally be allowed to drive a manual transmission vehicle. This can significantly limit your choices when purchasing your own car or hiring vehicles in future.

We only offer new driver lessons using a manual transmission car, but can provide refresher lessons in an automatic if you supply your own vehicle.
How will you know when I'm ready to take the practical test?
We will assess your performance with our Learner Progress Logs which we use to track your progress. We ensure that we meet data protection laws and keep each seperate learner's files private and confidential.
How can I pay for my lessons?
We will happily accept Cash or Bank Transfers as suitable payment for lessons and courses.
Will I use my instructor's car on the practical test?
It is advised that you use the instructor's car during the practical test, we also suggest that you book a lesson before the test so that you can go over anything that you may be unsure of and settle your nerves ahead of time.
Once I've passed the theory test, how long is this valid for?
Once you have completed and passed your theory test, this is valid for up to two years from the date you passed it.
What if I need to cancel or rearrange a driving lesson?
If you need to cancel or rearrange a booked lesson, please feel free to contact us and we will be happy to cancel or re-schedule another booking.
Will I be charged for missing a lesson at short notice?
It is important that if you need to cancel a booking, you must do so 36 hours prior to the lesson, otherwise you will be charged as this time could be used to provide a lesson to other learners.

For any information, please give us a call or contact us
